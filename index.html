FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers! Find quick information about our services and process below.

We provide a comprehensive range of digital services including graphic design (logo, branding, UI/UX), photography (product, event, portrait), videography (corporate, promotional, social media videos), and web design & development (custom websites, e-commerce, responsive design).

Our process is collaborative and transparent, involving six key phases: Discovery & Planning, Concept & Design, Development & Production, Review & Refinement, Delivery & Launch, and optional ongoing Support & Optimization. We keep you informed and involved every step of the way.

Project timelines are highly dependent on the scope and complexity. For instance, a basic logo design might take a few weeks, while a comprehensive web development project with video content could take several months. We'll provide a detailed and realistic timeline after our initial consultation and project assessment.

Client satisfaction is our top priority. We build in review and refinement stages throughout our process to ensure your feedback is incorporated. If you're not satisfied, we'll work closely with you to understand your concerns and make the necessary revisions until the work meets your expectations and project goals.
